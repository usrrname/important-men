Brad is always at least ten minutes early to any meeting. He runs ten miles every Saturday morning and always puts the toilet seat down.

He has sensitive skin and eats everything except olives and mayo. There is at least five shades of half-used bubblegum pink lipstick in his makeup drawer. Brad used to play music in a punk band but considers it a young man’s game today. Brad says he works in IT, not "the tech space".

Brad's expression at the thought of durian, olives, or mayo. Photo: Gabriella Hileman

In his free time you'll find Brad roaming around Final Fantasy XIV with a level 70 monk and a vanity pet pig, online shopping for wigs or trolling Chinatown for cheap eats. Brad used to dress up more but he works a eighty-hour week and it’s too hard to get up the hill to his tiny condo with 4 inch stillettos on.
