Devin wakes up at 7am every morning and skates to the woodwork studio. He has a strong eye for identifying street art and scrawled statements in the tiniest of places. You have probably not heard of his favourite band.


When he's not in his studio you will find him thumping away at his classic iBook or writing rap about rapping on an iBook. Devin has probably done over than a decade of school at famous schools and he continues to study, read, and write, despite the longwinded bureaucracy of granting councils and tumultuous rejections from slow turnover art programs.
